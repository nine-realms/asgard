## Key Finding

The SELECT 1 duplication complaint (Sonnet's -1 in Arm C) is **gone** — no model mentioned it in C2. Fix was successful. Score held at 43.5 avg because both Sonnet and Opus independently surfaced a deeper structural issue: the **prose section boundary between MFA-E and Step 0** is where loops fail to start.

Both models gave the same root cause independently:
- The MFA→Loop handoff is a navigational prose instruction ("begin the Odin Loop at Step 0"), not a gate
- The section break creates a natural stop point for the LLM
- Multiple "Do not stop here" warnings scattered across 70 lines are symptoms of this structural problem

Opus's concrete fix suggestion: merge MFA-E and Step 0's loop-entry gate into a single continuous section — no header break between them. Sonnet's fix: a flat imperative block at the top with zero interleaved conditionals.
