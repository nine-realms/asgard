## Changes in v0.12.2

5 benchmark-driven spec fixes:

1. **Intent Router → Step 0 pointer** — All 3 Odin Loop rows now say `→ Step 0`
2. **Missing Step 2 explanation** — One-line note: "Step 2 was merged into Step 1 in v0.11.0"
3. **Step 8 Frigg gate hole closed** — Pre-commit checklist now verifies `review-frigg` passed
4. **Non-overridable behaviors promoted** — Converted to `> ⚠️` callout
5. **Gate Registry updated** — Step 8 row includes `review-frigg`

## Score Movement Analysis

- **Opus +3**: Biggest gainer. The Step 2 note resolved its prior numbering confusion (no longer flagged as a failure mode). The Frigg gate closure was correctly identified in its Step 8 gate enumeration. The `⚠️` callout boosted its MFA Clarity score from 8 → 10.
- **Sonnet +1**: Was already top scorer. Gained on MFA Clarity (8 → 9) — the `⚠️` callout helped. Still flagged the same structural issues (118 lines of preamble, "conversational by default" framing).
- **GPT-5.4 ±0**: Scores unchanged. Its weaknesses are in depth of structural analysis, not missing information.
- **GPT-5.3-Codex ±0**: Scores unchanged. Surface-level analysis pattern persists regardless of spec clarity improvements.
