## Interpretation

**Overall: +0.3 avg.** The v0.9.3 MFA hardening delivered modest but real score improvement, with the largest gain coming from Codex (+1 on MFA clarity). More importantly, the qualitative shift is significant:

- **No model flagged the CRITICAL callout issue** — the merged MFA header fix worked
- **All 4 models referenced the routing table** in their analysis — confirming it's processable and helpful
- **All 4 models referenced the materialization checklist** — the 3/4 convergence finding from v0.9.2 is now addressed
- **New convergence shifted to deeper structural issues**: the 110-line MFA→Step 0 gap and silent MFA execution are now the top findings

**The file has stabilized at 44+ avg (Good/Excellent boundary).** The remaining improvement opportunities are increasingly structural (reorganizing sections for linear reading flow) rather than content issues. These align with Tier 3 (prose compression) — moving reference material to appendices would simultaneously reduce the MFA→Step 0 gap and compress the file.

**Key insight for next iteration**: The most impactful single change would be moving the Runtime Gate details, Task Sizing, and Verification Ledger sections to *after* the Loop steps. This would make the reading path MFA → Step 0 → Step 1 → ... with no interruptions. Two models independently recommended this.
