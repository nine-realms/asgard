### Interpretation

The terse overlay has a **slightly negative effect** (−0.50 avg):
- **Opus** is unaffected — already concise in Arm A
- **Sonnet** is unaffected — Gates improved 9→10 but other dimensions held
- **GPT-5.4 and Codex both drop MFA Clarity 9→8** — the concise constraint may cause these models to process the MFA section more shallowly, surfacing more structural criticisms (branching complexity, cognitive load) that they glossed over in the verbose Arm A response

**Conclusion:** The terse overlay does not improve benchmark scores on v0.10.8. The "just be concise" hypothesis is rejected — brevity alone doesn't help, and may slightly hurt GPT-family models' MFA analysis depth.
