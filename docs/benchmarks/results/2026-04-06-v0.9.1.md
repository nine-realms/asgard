## Interpretation

**Overall: Flat (-0.5 avg).** The v0.9.1 restructuring maintained comprehension quality while reducing the file by 68 lines (716→648, -9.5%). The skills factoring didn't degrade model understanding — models correctly identified `skill("odin-recall")` as an advisory dependency and understood the hard-dep pattern for `odin-review-prompts`.

**Opus upgrade (+5):** The largest movement, but primarily reflects the scoring methodology change (proper standalone run vs self-assessment). Opus produced the deepest analysis of any model in either benchmark run.

**Sonnet slight regression (-3):** Likely reflects that the v0.8.0-specific weaknesses Sonnet flagged (missing baseline gate query, no loop-start marker) have been fixed, so its critique had less surface area. Sonnet's unique insights remain the most actionable (stale ledger data, ambiguity gate accuracy).

**Key takeaway:** The file is stable at "Good" (43/50 average). The remaining improvement opportunities are structural (ungated silent zone, continuation boundary, investigation escape hatch) rather than clarity issues. These are addressable without major restructuring.
